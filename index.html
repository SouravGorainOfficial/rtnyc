Welcome to RTNYC

Rabindranath Thakur National Youth Council(RTNYC) has been registered under the Public Charitable Trust Act 1882 under section 17 vide Reg. No. IV-00197. RTNYC is also certified by ISO 9001:2008. RTNYC is also registered under Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Govt. of India.

The training time in RTNYC is divided into theory and practical sessions. Apart from regular computer sessions, students are allotted unlimited machine time to exercise their minds, boost their creativity and increase their level of curiosity .This builds-up the self confidence of the students and making them confident professionals.
